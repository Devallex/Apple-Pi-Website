Who we Are

Located in the town of Guilford, CT and surrounding communities, Apple Pi Robotics is an FRC team with the goal of bringing real-world experience in STEM to high school students. They are aided by our team mentors, who come from a wide background of engineering experience. Still, participating in the team is not all about building robots. There are opportunities in managing the business as an NPO, and we also take pride in helping to build our community through frequent events and fundraisers.

Check out the about us page to learn more about who we are and what we do.
